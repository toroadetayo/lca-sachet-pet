## Title
Cradle-to-Grave Life Cycle Assessment of Sachet and PET Bottled Drinking Water in Lagos, Nigeria

## Abstract (draft)
Sachet water is widely consumed in West Africa yet its lifecycle environmental impacts are poorly quantified. This study provides a cradle-to-grave life cycle assessment (LCA) comparing sachet water and PET bottled water for Lagos, Nigeria, focusing on global warming potential (GWP), water depletion, and cumulative energy demand. Using open-source LCI proxies, field-relevant inventory assumptions, and uncertainty/sensitivity analyses, we identify packaging production and end-of-life management as primary hotspots and evaluate mitigation scenarios including increased recycling and lightweight packaging.
